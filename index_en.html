    "We want to be a dynamic environment in which to promote professionalism, skills and, thanks to coordination in the field, to create added value for the territory by highlighting the potential of the University of Bergamo's students".

    JUNIOR ENTERPRISE BERGAMO

    WHAT WE ARE OFFERING

    JEB offers IT-oriented services to make the most of the context in which it is located and the expertise of its members.
    In particular, we want to offer companies the following services:

    • Development of management applications
    • Office automation
    • Web and mobile application development
    • Branding
    • Construction of marketing strategies

    OUR SERVICES

    IT Area

    The core business of JEB.
    The IT area is responsible for providing the customer with a guide in choosing the most suitable tools and information technologies for each specific case.

    Commercial Area

    This area takes care of the entrepreneurial part of JEB by dedicating itself to the client for the duration of the project and managing its formalization from lead generation to the contract.

    HR Area

    They take care of the members of the Junior Enterprise by forming the associates and by allocating the resources for the projects. It is also their job to manage the recruitment campaigns for the new students of UniBG who want to join our JEB project.

    Marketing Area

    This area is in charge of social planning for the Junior Enterprise and its clients. Taking care of the image of JEB by managing its social profiles and dealing with the drafting of marketing plans for companies.
